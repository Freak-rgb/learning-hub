About the Learning Hub

Our Mission

To empower students through peer-to-peer mentorship, ensuring that knowledge is shared freely and every student has the support they need to succeed.

Our Vision

To create a self-sustaining academic community where senior students guide juniors, fostering a culture of collaboration and excellence across the campus.
